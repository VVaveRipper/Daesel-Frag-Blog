Daesel Frag Development Blog Release!!

May 29, 2025

Welcome to the official development blog for Daesel Frag! Here I'll be sharing regular updates on my progress, design decisions, and sneak peeks of what's to come with the game.

First of all, I would have to show some of the core aspects of the game. To do this, I'll give a brief rundown of what the game is about, the goal of the game and the inspiration behind all of it.

  • What is Daesel Frag?

    • Battle a horde of enemies in a round-based, hack-n-slash, rogue-like style gameplay. The main playable character of the game is Ryujin, but he isn't the only playable character available. As you play the game more, you will notice that there are several features that you can unlock after completing several runs.

  • Goal of the game

    • The goal of the game is to survive as long as you can until you meet your inevitable demise (similar to CoD zombies). After you've been defeated, your progress is reset to 0 and you are sent back to the main hub world called The Silent's Retreat. Inside of this hub world, you can allocate stat points to grow stronger for your next playthroughs and interact with stations that provide items through shopping and upgrade gear. Rinse and repeat to unlock the best possible stats for the character(s).

    As you play, you will receive in-game EXP points to gain special skills and abilities to help you during your playthrough. As you progress through rounds, enemies get tougher to beat. After every 10 rounds, a special boss enemy will spawn (Subject to change). Once you've reached 100 rounds, the player is met with 3 choices: "Defeat a final boss and receive legendary grade gear and double the EXP for your current playthrough. (Very hard)" or "End the current playthrough and receive the according EXP and materials" or "Continue the playthrough in an endless mode."

  • Inspirations

    • In this section of the blog, I'll be sharing some of the main inspirations behind the development of the game.

    One of the biggest inspirations for this game is the Legend of heroes series, also known as the Trails series.

    One of the reasons why I love the Trails series is because of the various returning characters that you can meet throughout the series. That aspect alone is something that I wanted to implement in this game. It gives a sense of nostalgia to the player when they see a familiar face from the previous games.

    Stay tuned for more updates as we progress with development!

    Character Design Insights

    June 5, 2025

    Today we're excited to share some insights into our character design process for Daesel Frag! Our art team has been working tirelessly to create characters that are not only visually striking but also deeply connected to the game's lore.

    Each character in Daesel Frag is designed with a unique silhouette and color palette that makes them instantly recognizable in the heat of battle. We've paid special attention to their weapons and abilities, ensuring they feel natural extensions of each character's personality and background story.

    We'll be revealing more characters in the coming weeks, so stay tuned for more updates!

    Combat System Preview

    June 12, 2025

    This week, we're giving you a sneak peek at Daesel Frag's combat system! Our goal has been to create a system that's easy to pick up but offers depth for skilled players.

    The combat in Daesel Frag focuses on fast-paced action with a mix of light and heavy attacks, dodges, and special abilities unique to each character. As players progress through rounds, they'll unlock more powerful skills and combo opportunities.

    We've implemented a dynamic difficulty scaling system that ensures enemies remain challenging as players become more powerful. This creates a satisfying progression curve that rewards skill improvement without becoming frustratingly difficult.

    Enemy Types Revealed

    June 20, 2025

    Today we're pulling back the curtain on the various enemy types you'll face in Daesel Frag! The game features a diverse roster of opponents, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

    From nimble assassins that dart around the battlefield to hulking brutes that can soak up massive damage, you'll need to adapt your tactics based on the enemy composition in each round. Some special enemies even have environmental abilities that can drastically change the battlefield.

    Boss enemies appear every 10 rounds and feature complex, multi-phase encounters that will test your mastery of the game's mechanics. Defeating these formidable foes rewards players with rare gear and substantial experience points.

    Characters

    Ryujin St-Ishtar

    Rogue class

    Ryujin St-Ishtar

    Rogue class

    Background

    Born into a military family, Ryujin enlisted at 18 and quickly rose through the ranks due to his exceptional combat skills. After a classified mission went wrong, he was betrayed by his commanding officers and left for dead.

    Abilities

    Ryujin possesses enhanced reflexes and tactical awareness from experimental treatments. His signature weapons are dual plasma pistols that can combine into a more powerful rifle.

    Personality

    Despite his dark past, Ryujin maintains a strong sense of justice and loyalty to those he trusts. He's calculating in combat but can be reckless when pursuing personal vendettas.

    Role in Story

    As players progress through the game, they discover that Ryujin's quest for revenge unveils a larger conspiracy within the Daesel Frag tournament, forcing him to decide between personal vengeance and the greater good.

    Danika Mizuno

    Samurai class

    Danika Mizuno

    Samurai class

    Background

    Raised in the digital underworld of New Shanghai, Nova was a child prodigy who hacked her first corporate database at age 12. After discovering evidence of dangerous AI technology being developed by the Daesel Corporation, she dedicated herself to exposing their activities.

    Abilities

    Nova deploys autonomous combat drones and uses a specialized neural interface to control multiple technological systems simultaneously. Her ultimate ability allows her to temporarily hijack enemy weapons and technology.

    Personality

    Confident and analytical, Nova approaches combat like a complex puzzle to be solved. She has a dry sense of humor and forms unexpected friendships with other competitors who share her suspicions about the tournament.

    Role in Story

    Nova's investigation into the Daesel Frag AI leads to discoveries about its true purpose, potentially setting her on a collision course with Axel as their separate quests intersect in unexpected ways.

    Clint Hubert

    Brawler class

    Clint Hubert

    Brawler class

    Background

    The product of a classified super-soldier program, Magnus escaped his creators and has been living off the grid for years. The Daesel Frag tournament provides him both the challenge he craves and the resources to stay hidden from those who would recapture him.

    Abilities

    Magnus possesses superhuman strength, accelerated healing, and a specialized exoskeleton that amplifies his already formidable abilities. His signature weapon is a massive gravity hammer that can create devastating shockwaves.

    Personality

    Stoic and imposing, Magnus speaks little but observes everything. He follows a strict personal code of honor in combat and respects opponents who demonstrate skill and courage, regardless of their physical capabilities.

    Role in Story

    As players advance through the tournament, Magnus transitions from intimidating final boss to potential ally, as his own investigation into the tournament organizers aligns with the protagonists' goals.

    About Daesel Frag

    Game Overview

    Daesel Frag is an innovative game that blends action, strategy, and immersive storytelling. Set in a unique world where players will encounter challenging obstacles and engage with a rich narrative.

    The world of Daesel Frag is set in a futuristic environment where technology and ancient powers collide. Players will uncover the mysteries of this world as they progress through the game.

    Gameplay

    Featuring fast-paced action and strategic elements, Daesel Frag offers a unique gaming experience that challenges players' skills and decision-making abilities.

    Development Team

    Daesel Frag is being developed by a passionate team dedicated to creating an exceptional gaming experience. Our team consists of talented designers, developers, artists, and writers working together to bring this vision to life.
